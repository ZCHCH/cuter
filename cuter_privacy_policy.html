Cuter Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 29, 2026

Introduction

Cuter is an AI-powered image and video generation application operated by an independent developer. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how uploaded media may be processed, and what choices you have when using Cuter.

By using Cuter, you acknowledge that your information may be processed as described in this policy so we can provide the app, maintain reliability, support user-requested AI generation features, and respond to support or legal obligations.

Information We Collect

We collect information that is reasonably necessary to provide Cuter and support the app experience.

  • Account details and sign-in information, including guest access, device-based access, or Apple-based access data when applicable.
  • Content you choose to upload, save, or manage in the app, including photos, videos, prompts, reference media, uploaded media URLs, generated outputs, and related task records.
  • Usage, device, and diagnostic information such as device type, operating system, app version, crash logs, IP address, and service logs.
  • Subscription, purchase, membership, and entitlement status needed to manage premium access and restore purchases.
  • Support communications that you choose to send to us.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to operate Cuter, provide requested AI generation features, improve product quality, and protect the service from misuse.

  • Provide AI image generation, AI video generation, image expansion, action imitation, saved content access, and account-related functionality.
  • Upload and process user-selected reference media only when needed to complete the feature requested by the user.
  • Show task status, generated results, purchase status, membership status, and account history.
  • Maintain stability, security, abuse prevention, fraud prevention, and performance monitoring.
  • Respond to support requests, legal obligations, and legitimate operational needs.

Cloud and AI Processing

Some content you upload may be processed by cloud-based services when that processing is necessary to deliver the feature you requested.

For example, when you choose an AI generation feature and upload a reference image or video, Cuter may upload that media to cloud object storage and send the relevant media URL, prompt, and task parameters to a cloud AI processing provider solely to produce the requested result.

Cuter does not sell uploaded media, generated outputs, prompts, or face-related information.

Facial Information in Uploaded Content

Cuter does not scan your device for face data, enroll face templates, identify people, authenticate users with face data, or build facial profiles.

When you choose to upload a photo or video for an AI generation feature, that media may contain a face. In that situation, the facial information present in the uploaded media is processed only as part of the image or video you selected.

  • What is collected: user-selected photos or videos, related upload URLs, generation prompts, task records, and generated results. Cuter does not separately extract or store biometric face templates, face geometry, face identifiers, or Face ID data.
  • How it is used: to complete the AI image, AI video, image expansion, action imitation, or similar generation request that you start in the app.
  • What it is not used for: Cuter does not use facial information to identify users, authenticate users, track users across apps or websites, build advertising profiles, or train unrelated models.
  • Third-party sharing: uploaded media URLs may be sent to cloud AI processing providers solely to produce the requested generation result. They may not be used by Cuter for identity recognition, advertising profiles, or unrelated model training.
  • Where it is stored: uploaded media and generated results may be stored in cloud object storage, while generation task metadata may be stored in Cuter service records so the app can show task status, results, and account history.
  • Retention: uploaded media, task records, and generated results are retained only as long as needed to provide the service, maintain account history, support reliability and security, or meet legal obligations. Users may delete generated works in the app or contact support to request account or data deletion.

Photo Library, Camera, and Microphone Access

Cuter requests Photo Library access so you can choose reference images to upload for AI image, AI video, image expansion, and action imitation generation. Cuter requests Camera access if you choose to take a photo for upload. Cuter requests Microphone access only when audio recording is needed for supported video creation or editing features.

These permissions are used only when you choose to use the related feature.

Third-Party Services

Cuter may use third-party infrastructure or service providers for cloud storage, AI generation processing, payment processing through Apple, analytics, diagnostics, security, and customer support. These providers may process information only as needed to provide their services to Cuter or to complete the feature requested by the user.

Auto-renewable subscriptions and in-app purchases are processed by Apple. Cuter receives purchase and entitlement status needed to unlock or restore premium access.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably needed to provide the service, maintain account history, show generated results, support reliability and security, comply with law, resolve disputes, or preserve legitimate business records.

When retention is no longer necessary, data may be deleted, de-identified, or anonymized according to operational and legal requirements.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may request access to, correction of, deletion of, restriction of, or export of your personal information.

You may delete generated works in the app where available, stop using the app, manage account actions from the product, or contact us directly for privacy-related requests.

Deleting your Cuter account does not automatically cancel an Apple subscription. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your App Store account settings.

Children

Cuter is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to use online services without consent. If you believe a child has provided personal information without appropriate consent, please contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and operational measures designed to protect information processed by Cuter. However, no internet or cloud service can guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the app, legal requirements, or operational practices. The updated version will be posted through appropriate product or website channels, and the “Last Updated” date will be revised.
